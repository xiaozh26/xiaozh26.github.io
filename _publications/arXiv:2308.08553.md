excerpt: 'This study presents an upgraded urban water storage system that integrates solar panels over surface reservoirs and water channels to achieve water conservation, power generation, carbon reduction, and water quality improvement. A prototype monitoring and control system was built using Python and Raspberry Pi, enabling automated data collection and real-time analysis during field experiments. Results demonstrate significant potential for large-scale environmental and economic benefits in megacities like Beijing.'
